About the Initiative

Organized by the Tech Titans Association, Department of Information Technology, Paperless Day 2026 is held in observance of Forest Day. Our goal is to promote forest conservation, sustainable development, and responsible digital practices on campus.

On this day, participants will completely abandon paper in favor of digital platforms for all communication, documentation, and presentations. Join us in making a sustainable impact through three distinct, simultaneous digital challenges!

🌱 Event Categories & Rules

Three simultaneous events. Choose your expertise.

💻

Ink Free Web

An offline web design competition focused on creating interactive sites that promote forest conservation and green technology.

📱

Inkless Ideas

An offline content summarization challenge conducted entirely on mobile devices to enhance eco-awareness and digital efficiency.

💡

Think Inkless

A digital idea (paperless paper) presentation showcasing innovative technological solutions to reduce paper usage in daily life.

General Guidelines

  • Absolutely NO paper allowed. Using paper for notes, sketches, or scripts will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Format: All events are strictly for individual participants. No teams are permitted.
  • Capacity Caps: The event is capped at 150 participants total across all events. Register early to secure your spot!
  • Simultaneous Schedule: All three events occur at the same time. You may only register for ONE event.
